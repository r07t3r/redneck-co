Our Story

Texas Born.
Texas Proud.

Founded in Austin in 2010, Redneck.co was built on a simple belief — every Texan deserves access to honest, high-quality financial protection and smart investment strategies.

Our Mission

Protecting Texas,
One Family at a Time

We started Redneck.co because we were tired of seeing Texans get sold overpriced insurance and underperforming investments by advisors who didn't understand the Texas way of life.

We're not a call center. We're not a national chain. We're a Texas company, staffed by Texans, serving the communities we call home. From the Hill Country to the Gulf Coast, from El Paso to Beaumont — we've got Texas covered.

Our name says it all. We're plain-spoken, hardworking, and we don't dress up bad products with fancy language. If it's not right for you, we'll tell you straight.
